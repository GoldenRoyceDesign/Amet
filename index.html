AMET: Marine Colleges in Chennai For a Post Sea Courses

AMET City College, a unit of AMET University, embarked in to the area of Post Sea Training to enrich the knowledge and expertise of seafarers and Maritime Professionals.

AMET City College is a dynamic and innovative institution committed to providing high-quality education and fostering a stimulating learning environment. With a strong focus on academic excellence, we offer a diverse range of programs designed to meet DG Shipping requirements as well as the evolving needs of our students and prepare them for successful careers.
