Dr.Deepa Rajesh

Vice-President (Academics)

Vice President (Academics) and Executive Director of AMET Business School, AMET University, AMET City College Chennai has rich academic, research and administrative experience. She specialized in Human Resources and System and completed her Doctorate in University of Madras, Chennai. She also serve as a Director, Human Resource Development Centre, AMET University and Nodal officer for AMET University Academic and Administrative Development Centre (AADC), Association of Indian University.

She published more number of articles in Peer reviewed/Scopus indexed journals and books. Organized International/ National Conferences/ Seminars in the recent advancements towards sustainability. She served as a resource person in National and International Conferences/Workshop and Faculty Development Programmes. She served as editorial and review board member of peer reviewed/Scopus indexed journals. She served as Board of Studies Member of Guru Nanak College, Chennai. She received Best Citizen of India Gold Medal award and Young Researcher award for Outstanding Contribution in the Management Research Award. She served as visiting professor in University of Madras to educate the working professional in the weekend and external examiner for Tamil Nadu Open University and SRM University, Chennai.
