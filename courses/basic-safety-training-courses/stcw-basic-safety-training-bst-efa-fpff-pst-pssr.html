STCW Basic Safety Training Course (BST= EFA+FPFF+PST+PSSR)

Basic Safety training (BST) consists of Four courses namely,1.PSSR,2.PST,3.EFA,4.FPFF.

This course is designed as per STCW requirements and guidelines provided by DG Shipping.

STCW Basic Safety Training Course Description:

  • PSSR:This course is designed to enhance knowledge,understanding and proficiency about ship familiarization,Different types of ships,safe working practices,Interpersonal relationships,Team building,Team working skills and conflict resolution.This course also teaches effective communication skills,environmental protection,and following emergency procedures.
  • PST:It deals with types of emergencies,different emergency drills,emergency stations,boat stations.Parts of survival crafts,launching procedures and survival at sea.
  • EFA:A candidate should understand basic parts of the body, Human Anatomy and Functions, Positioning of Casualty specially an Unconscious state. Resuscitation Techniques, Bleeding,Management of Shock ,Burns and Scalds, andRescue and Transport of Casualty.
  • FPFF:The objective of the course is to understand the theory of fire,fire prevention and protection.Ship construction and arrangements.Operation of Different types of Fire-extinguishing Systems,Fire pumps,cooling systems,Fire Fighting methods and teams that is assigned with various duties during the fire emergency.

Course Subjects / Topics Include:

  • PSSR-Personal Safety and Social Responsibility
  • PST-Personal Survival Techniques
  • EFA-Elementary First Aid
  • FPFF-Fire Prevention and Fire Fighting

This Course is Mandatory for:

Those who want to sail on board merchant ships,Cruise vessels,oil rigs etc,.and also to apply for the Indian CDC(Continuous Discharge Certificate)which is a mandatory Document required for sailing on board Merchant Ships.

Prerequisites :

Passport, INDoS Certificate,Medical Certificate from DG Approved Doctor
